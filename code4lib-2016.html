Data Loading

  • One Islandora Object per Oral History
  • Many data streams for each object:
    1. OBJ: XML produced by the OHMS Synchronizer
    2. TN: A photo of the interviewee
    3. MODS & DC: Metadata goodness generated by Islandora
    4. TRANSCRIPT: PDF of the transcript
    5. BIO: A plain text bio of the interviewee
    6. NAMES: A CSV listing all the names the interviewee referenced
