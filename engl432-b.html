Use single quotes


          <link title='This is a "title"'/>

Special Characters

Use a character entity


          <link title="This is a &quot;title&quot;"/>

Special Characters

  • Starts with an ampersand &
  • Then some letters (quot, lt)
  • Ends with a semicolon

Special Characters

XML defines four character entities

  • &amp; - Ampersand &
  • &lt; - Less than <
  • &gt; - Greater than >
  • &quot; - Double quotation mark "

You can also define others
